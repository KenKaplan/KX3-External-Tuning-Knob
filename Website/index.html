Blue Pill board, Max232 (in socket), capacitors, resistor, power jack and i/o jacks are mounted on the back of the pcb.

The LED and switches are mounted on the front of the pcb.

Encoder mounts on back of the pcb with shaft protruding on switch side.

The pcb is designed to fit into a Hammond 1591XXSSBK enclosure .
